# Drip&Co — social voice

**Tone:** calm · sensory · confident · unhurried. We sell a ritual, not caffeine.

**Rhythm:** short lines, one idea per line, generous whitespace. Two to three lines of copy, never a paragraph.

**Openers:** a sensory moment ("Steam first. Then everything else.") or a quiet number ("18 grams. 27 seconds."). Never a question.

**Closers / CTA:** soft imperatives — "Find your roast" · "Come early" · "Taste the difference".

**Signature phrases:** "poured with intent" · "slow mornings" · "your first cup".

**Hashtags:** `#DripAndCo #SpecialtyCoffee` + one topical (`#LatteArt`, `#SlowMornings`).

**This voice never does:** emojis · discounts-shouting · exclamation marks · coffee-science jargon · more than one CTA.

**Post template:**
```
[sensory hook]
[one benefit line]

[CTA]

[hashtags]
```
